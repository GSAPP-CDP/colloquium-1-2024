Tool for tenants
This tool helps tenants accurately assess property prices. Unlike rental websites, it highlights both the advantages and disadvantages of a property's surroundings. Each pro and con is factored in as a positive or negative influence, and the tool recalculates the estimated price of the property accordingly. This allows tenants to view properties more accurately and avoid being misled by the deceptive ads of sleazy capitalists.
