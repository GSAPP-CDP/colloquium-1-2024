Empirical Space

Empirical space represents the everyday structures and tools that shape our cities, like those captured in street view images. These images reveal the tangible, often overlooked elements—buildings, roads, and infrastructure—that define urban color through the physical realities of our daily environment.

Image Space

Image Space reflects urban color schemes on social platforms like Instagram and TikTok, shaping public perceptions and driving rapid shifts in color trends. Social media serves as a feedback loop, where trends and user content continually influence urban color choices.
