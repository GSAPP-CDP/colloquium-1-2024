John Masataka Xianfeng Jiang

John Masataka Xianfeng Jiang is a Tokyo/New York-based architect and project manager with over seven years of professional experience at Kengo Kuma & Associates, where he has led teams on a wide range of projects, including luxury towers, resorts, private residences, and high-end furniture. He has been responsible for global architectural projects, bridging the confluence of technology, design, and management. Notable completed work includes Endo at the Rotunda (London), Aman Miami Beach (Florida), LA Brooktree House (California), and Alberni Tower (Vancouver). John also serves as an assistant researcher at the Center for Spatial Research in New York. He received his master's degree in architecture from Hokkaido University in Japan and master's degree in computation from Columbia University in the United States.
