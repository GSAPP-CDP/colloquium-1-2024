Lincoln Square

High-rise apartments in Lincoln Square offer stunning views of Central Park, the Hudson River, and Manhattan’s skyline. Higher floors provide panoramic, unobstructed vistas, making them highly desirable, while views on lower floors can vary significantly. Whether you're drawn to the vibrant arts scene, the green spaces of Central Park, or the convenience of city living, Lincoln Park offers something for everyone.

The Toulaine

The Toulaine is a highly sought-after apartment building located at 130 W 67th St, New York, NY 10023, in the vibrant and culturally rich neighborhood of Lincoln Park, NYC. This area is renowned for its blend of urban sophistication and historic charm, making it one of the most desirable places to live in Manhattan. With its proximity to cultural landmarks, excellent dining and shopping options, and a strong sense of community, it's no wonder that apartments in this building are highly coveted.
