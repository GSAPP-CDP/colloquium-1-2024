MANAS BHATIA

My practice focuses on the use of AI and Generative Design to explore new styles of architecture inspired by nature.

We see a lot of Generative AI images here and there. But these can be based on nature and growth, and completely change the notion of how we design the built environment.

The existing tools are not that organic...

Can Generative AI change the architects' workflow to be as organic as nature?

Humans don't belong in concrete/glass boxes!
What would it mean to design buildings that were more like ant colonies, or more like forests?
