Design Fiction

In the future smart city, Mr. Wang's travel experience is completely overturned. Every morning, his XR navigation system will provide him with the best parking plan based on real-time traffic, parking lot occupancy and geographic information. Through holographic projection, Mr. Wang can choose the route that saves the most time and money.
When he drives, the vehicle interacts seamlessly with the city infrastructure and Robotaxi to ensure smooth travel. When approaching the destination, the XR system will mark the vacant parking space on the car window to guide him to park quickly. The whole process is efficient and time-saving, and Mr. Wang no longer has to worry about finding a parking space. Every day in the future, he can enjoy this intelligent travel convenience..
